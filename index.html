Message Of The Day

Help Opens in a new window
  • Options Message Of The Day

  • Weekly OWL maintenance

    Starting October 23rd, OWL will be unavailable briefly on Sunday mornings from 6:00am to 6:10am ET for routine system maintenance. Please schedule assignments, assessments, and other work outside of this time.

    Looking for your course in OWL?

    Your courses should appear across the top of OWL or under the Sites menu when they are available to you.

    You can also check your "Official Course Enrolments" page:

    1. Log into OWL
    2. Go to "Membership" on the left
    3. Go to "My Official Course Enrolments"

    Your official course enrolments will be listed (with links to the course site, if available).

    If you've changed courses or sections recently, you should see the change in OWL within 24 hours.

    Course sites were unpublished on September 20, 2022

    As previously communicated, OWL course sites for Fall 2020, Winter 2021, and Summer 2021 courses were unpublished on September 20, 2022. Only instructors and site maintainers have access to their unpublished course sites.

    For more information about the course site unpublishing process, please see the OWL Help course sites policies page. If you have any questions, please contact the WTS Helpdesk.

    Instructors: Course Site Templates  are available in OWL

    The eLearning Technology Team (ELT) and Instructional Technology Resource Centre (ITRC) have developed OWL templates for course sites. The templates include common OWL tools, course homepage banners, and organized Lessons ready to be populated with content. There are two designs to choose from: Basic and Advanced. You can have a template applied to your course site by completing the request form.

    If you have been using the Basic or Advanced OWL Templates in an OWL site prior to May 1, 2021, there are updates available to address the new formatting. Please see this article about the OWL Template Updates.

    If you're using custom CSS in your OWL sites, changes to Lessons at the beginning of May 2021 may have affected your CSS. Please see this article about what changes to make to your custom Lessons CSS.

    Instructors and Site Maintainers:

    Course sites can be set up for the Fall/Winter terms. Start by going to Worksite Setup and then "Create New Site". If you do not see a section that you are teaching, please confirm with your department that you are the "approved instructor for the correct term".
